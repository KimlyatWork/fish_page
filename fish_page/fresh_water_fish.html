Introduction

Freshwater fish are species that live in rivers, lakes, and streams. They are popular among aquarists due to their diverse colors, sizes, and behaviors.

Types of Freshwater Fish

White Cloud Mountain Minnow

White Cloud Mountain Minnows are small, peaceful fish with an attractive coloration of silver bodies and red fins. They are hardy and well-suited for cooler water temperatures. Scientific Name: Tanichthys albonubes

Koi

Koi are large, colorful fish often kept in outdoor ponds. They are admired for their striking patterns and can live for many years, often growing quite large.

Rosy Barb

Rosy Barbs are lively and colorful fish, with males displaying vibrant reddish-pink bodies and females being slightly less colorful. They are active swimmers and can add movement and energy to a cold water aquarium. Scientific Name: Pethia conchonius

Care Tips

  • Maintain proper water quality by performing regular water changes.
  • Feed your fish a balanced diet appropriate for their species.
  • Provide adequate space and proper tank mates to reduce stress.
